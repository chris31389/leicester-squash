Welcome to Leicester Squash Club, and thank you for visiting our website.

Founded in 1936, owned and run by its members, Leicester Squash Club is the longest established squash club in Leicestershire. We take pride in the quality of our five courts, reputed to be among the very best in the Midlands.

In 2009 the club achieved the England Squash and Racketball Charter Silver Award, which recognises it has excellent facilities, offers a welcoming environment, sets high standards in child protection, and works closely with local schools to develop the future generations of squash and racketball players.

Squash Opportunities at Leicester

We aim to provide opportunities for players of all standards. Five men’s teams compete in the Leicestershire squash leagues.

We have an active junior section and group coaching is available to junior members and non-members every Saturday morning and Tuesday evening.

Social squash is played by members of all ages and abilities and for those wanting competition there are internal leagues on a monthly promotion/relegation cycle. If you're new to squash have a look at our Beginners page.

During the summer the club hosts the very popular Leicestershire Squash Business League, this year sponsored by Pick Everard, one of the leading architectural and engineering partnerships in the country.

Doubles squash is well established at the club and there is an increasing interest among members in playing racketball.

How do I get involved or find out more?

Club Night on Fridays offers the opportunity for all members and prospective members to come along and play without prior arrangement.

The session runs from 17:00 till 20:00 and the first time that you attend it is completely FREE!

For more information contact one of our head coach Thomas Fairhurst 07837 067694

Club Gym – £18.00 A Month

Our gym was opened at Christmas 2013 and has a great selection of Lifefitness cardio-vascular equipment together with plenty of weights. Gym classes such as yoga, aerobics and performing arts are run during the evenings.

You can become a gym-only member for £18.00 a month. Contact info@leicestersquash.club for more information.

Bar And Social Scene

Our bar area overlooks all five courts and many a pleasant hour is spent by members chatting and watching the games being played from the comfort of the bar.

The club tours are legendary, many taking place in Europe. In recent years teams have travelled to Paris, Brussels, Budapest, Porto, Monaco, Rome, Brno and Dublin.

Premier Squash League

The 2016/17 season is our second year of competing in the Premier Squash League (PSL) in the UK. The team is lead by Commonwealth Medalist Daryl Selby. Visit our PSL page for more information.

We are proud of our club and its facilities and our members particularly value its convivial atmosphere.
