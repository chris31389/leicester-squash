General Rules of Membership

The Membership Year runs from 1 October to 30 September.

Membership fees are payable on 1 October.  Some categories (see below) can also be paid in monthly instalments by direct debit.

Membership is for a Membership Year and must be paid in full for each year.  Members should not cancel direct debits during the membership year.  Should a member cancel a direct debit for any reason the balance of the year’s membership must be paid immediately.  Reinstatement of membership will only be considered when all outstanding amounts are paid, together with a £75 administration fee.

Any member who has previously cancelled a direct debit will not be permitted to pay by monthly instalments when membership is reinstated.  All such reinstated membership fees must be paid on 1st October in full.

Members may bring guests into the club.  The member is responsible for the guest whilst the guest is in the club and for payment of any guest playing fees (see below).  Any individual guest may visit the club a maximum of 6 times in any Membership Year, irrespective of the member.  The member must ensure that his or her name and the guest’s name are both entered in the Guest Book in the bar.  By definition, a member can, under no circumstances, be a guest. A former member can also not be a guest at any time during the 2 Membership Years following cessation of membership.

Members of Leicester Squash Club Limited (as defined in the Memorandum & Articles of Association) exclude anyone under 18 years of age.

If a member wishes to cancel membership he or she may do so on 30 September in any Membership Year.  No refunds will be given under any circumstances.

New members joining the Membership Year will be required to pay pro rata from the date of joining to the end of the current Membership Year.

Membership Categories

Full Membership - £300

Adult full playing membership entitles the member to use all the facilities of the club.

Membership fees may be paid by Direct Debit at the rate of £26.00 per month (see General Rules of Membership above).

A discount of 10% of one fee will be given to any couple residing at the same address on 1 October in any Membership Year.  Proof of residential address will be required each year.

Juniors - £69

Junior full playing membership entitles the member to use all the facilities of the club except that Juniors under 16 are not permitted use of the Fitness Suite.

Junior membership is available to anyone who is under 19 years of age on 1 October in the relevant Membership Year.  Proof of age will be required.

Membership benefits include England Squash affiliation fees, a free T Shirt and half price off peak courts.

Full Time Student - £205

Full Time Student full playing membership entitles the member to use all the facilities of the club.

Full Time Student membership is available to anyone who is over 19 years of age and a full time student on 1 October in the relevant Membership Year.  Student ID will be required. There is no upper age limit.

Membership fees may be paid by Direct Debit at the rate of £18.00 per month (see General Rules of Membership above)

Country Membership - £205

Country full playing membership entitles the member to use all the facilities of the club.

Country membership is available to anyone who is resident 50 miles or more from the club on 1 October in the relevant Membership Year.  Proof of residential address will be required each year.

Membership fees may be paid by Direct Debit at the rate of £18.00 per month (see General Rules of Membership above)

Social - £64

Social non playing membership entitles the member to use the facilities of the club but not to play Squash or Squash57.

Family - £525

Family full playing membership entitles all members of the family to use all the facilities of the club.

Family membership is available to any family of two adults and any number of children under 19 who are resident at the same address on 1 October in the relevant Membership Year.  Proof of joint residential address and age of children will be required each year.

Membership fees may be paid by Direct Debit at the rate of £44.00 per month (see General Rules of Membership above)

Non Member Categories

Student Groups

Available to educational institutions for a minimum of 6 students.

Rates will be negotiated by the club directly with the institution.

Student fees may be paid by monthly Direct Debit (see General Rules of Membership above).

Students Group members are not Members of Leicester Squash Club Limited (see General Rules of Membership above).

Corporate

Available to employers for a minimum of 6 employees.

Rates will be negotiated by the club directly with the employer.

The full annual fee must be paid by the employer on 1 October.

The employer must nominate the individual employees on 1 October each year.

Employers may extend this to past employees only if at least 6 current employees have been nominated.

The nominated employees are not Members of Leicester Squash Club Limited (see General Rules of Membership above).

Fitness Suite only - £200

Fitness Suite only membership entitles the member to use the Fitness Suite, changing facilities and bar but not to play Squash or Squash57.

Membership fees may be paid by Direct Debit at the rate of £18.00 per month (see General Rules of Membership above)

Introductory offers are available from time to time.  Details may be obtained from the Club Manager.

The Fitness Suite only members are not Members of Leicester Squash Club Limited (see General Rules of Membership above).

Courts & Fees

Peak Court Times

Monday to Friday 12.30 – 13.30 & 17:00  - 19:45

Saturday 9.00 am to 1.00 pm (this change will be implemented during the 2016-17  the Membership Year)

Peak Court Fee - £5 per court for 45 minutes, £6 per court for 1 hour.

Off-peak Court Fee - £4 per court for 45 minutes, £5 per court for 1 hour.


England Squash - The annual affiliation fee (£6.50 for Adults and £4.50 Juniors) for all players is paid directly by the club to England Squash.


Joining Fee - A £25 joining fee is payable covering a gym induction and key fob (see below).


Key Fob - A special key fob that gives automatic entry to the club is provided to all members and players (other than to Juniors). Additional key fobs and replacements are available at a cost of £10.00.


Guests – A member who invites a guest to visit the club and to play must pay a Guest Fee of £3 in addition to the appropriate court fee. The fee should be paid when the court is booked and confirmation of payment made when entering the guest’s name in the guest book. If, for any reason, the guest fee is not paid, the member’s MyCourts account will be debited. Guests are not permitted use of the Fitness Suite. (See also General Rules of Membership above)

The above rules may be reviewed and amended at any time.
